What is the Pitt-Google Alerts Broker?

The Pitt-Google Broker (PGB) is a cloud-based, alert distribution service designed to provide near real-time processing for alerts from the Large Synoptic Survey Telescope (LSST). The project provides public access to all alerts and their value added data products, and is designed to support the development of downstream brokers built by external development teams. PGB is designed to run on the Google Cloud Platform (GCP) and is currently focused on processing alerts from the Zwicky Transient Facility (ZTF) and the LSST Alert Simulator (AlertSim) as a testing ground.
